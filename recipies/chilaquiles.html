Chilaquiles

Fried corn chips in spicy green salsa

This tasty dish is usually eaten as a breakfast meal but can be enjoyed any time of the day. With its cheap ingridients and little preparation time this dish cant be beat.

Although usually made with a tangy spicy green sauce it can be made with a more tarty red sauce

Ingridients

  • Green enchilada sauce(El pato is a popular brand wich can be found in green and red form easily)
  • Half and onion
  • Queso fresco
  • *optional* eggs
  • Corn chips

Steps

  1. Pour the red or green sauce into a pan and let come to a simmer.
  2. Next add onion slices for taste
  3. bring to a slight boil and add the corn chips
  4. Let sit for 10 min but keep watch so chips dont get too soggy
  5. Fianlly cook 2 eggs sunny side up
  6. Once done simply serve on a plate and add your queso fresco, Its that easy!
