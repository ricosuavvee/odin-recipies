Sopes

Tasty fried dough with black bean topping

This dish is made using pre-made dough disks wich we top with black beans served wth meat or plain cheese.

Because of its simplicity this dish is a go to for anyone looking for an easy authentic mexican dish thats sure to not dissapoint.

Ingridients

  • Pre-made dough disks (found in any local hispanic supermarket.)
  • Black beans(preferably pre-blended.)
  • Queso fresco
  • Your choice of meat
  • Queso oaxaca
  • 1 bottle of canola oil
  • *Optional* Green salsa

Steps

  1. Pour oil in a medium pot and set it to medium heat for about 5 min
  2. While the oil is preheating, open up your pre-blended beans and heat them up in the microwave for about 2 mins or until hot
  3. Prepare your green salsa if using
  4. Seasona and cook your choice of meat if using
  5. Once the oil is ready dip the sopes in the oil for no more than 30 secons
  6. Once ready spread some beans ontop
  7. Add some oaxaca cheese with some queso fresco
  8. Add your meat
  9. Add your salsa and your done! that eaasy
