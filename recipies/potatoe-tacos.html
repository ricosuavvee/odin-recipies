Tacos De Papa "Potato Tacos"

Tasty fried tortilla filled with a delicious potato filling

This mexican dish is prepared using reletevily cheap ingridients and can be cooked in less than an hour. Excellent for last minute meals and any left over potatoe can be stored up to a week in refrigiration for later use

Following the simple instructions below you to can make this awesome dish that will have you looking for them at your next local taco shop!.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 2 russet potatoes
  • Butter
  • Half a white onion
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Salt
  • Canola/vegetable oil
  • Cotija cheese
  • Water
  • Corn tortillas

STEPS

  1. Boil and peel russet potatoes.
  2. Add oinion to pot with butter and let butter melt.
  3. Add potatoes and begin smashing using a potato masher.
  4. Begin adding a pinch of salt followed by a bit of tomatoe sauce(enough to give it some color).
  5. Finally start grating some cotija cheese (dont be afraid to grate) as this is the key flavor ingridient.
